Title: Mental Health and Depression Treatment

Depression is a common mental health disorder characterized by persistent sadness, loss of interest, and impaired daily functioning. Symptoms may include changes in appetite, sleep disturbances, fatigue, and difficulty concentrating. Treatment options include psychotherapy, antidepressant medications, and lifestyle interventions. Cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) and selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors (SSRIs) are commonly used treatments.
